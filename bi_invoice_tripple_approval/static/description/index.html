Invoice Tripple Process

This app helps you to tripple approval process for Customer Invoice while validation of invoice.

Key Features:

  • Invoice approval process: Invoice Manager -> Finance Manager -> Director
  • After Install,This App will add approval buttons on customer invoice based on configuration in invoice setting. Let's say First order approval configure 5000 and invoice is more then 5000 then it require approval for assign user and then in second approval configure 6000 then if invoice amount more then 6000 it will require two lavel approval process for assign user then in third approval configure 10000 then if invoice amount more then 10000 it will require three lavel approval process for assign user.. .
  • You can set three step verification approval process on Invoice
  • You can set a limit amount on Invoice as Two and Three Step Verification
  • If Order's Total Amount exceeds the pre-defined limit,then Flow will be Two Step invoice validation process.
  • If Order's Total Amount exceeds the pre-defined limit of three step,then Flow will be Three Step validation process.

How To Use


Once module is installed Only User with "Manager(Accounting)" groups can Confirm invoice from 'First Approval' state and User with " Finance Manager (Accounting)" groups can Confirm invoice from 'Second Approval' state .


Setup & Configure

Configure Approval Amounts into invoice settings.


Enable "Double and Tripple Approval" to set Minimum Approval amounts.

Three Step can be enabled only when "Double Approval" is Enabled

If Amount Total of invoice will exceeds the limit of First Approval then flow will be Two Step

If Amount Total of invoice will exceeds the limit of Second Approval then flow will be Three Step

Invoice User who don't have any Approval Group .


Invoice will be into To Approve state after validate, because Total Amount of order is greater tha pre-defined First Approval Limit


Accounting Manager : Included into Accounting Manager(Accounting) group


invoice will be into Second Approval state after first approval, because Total Amount of invoice is greater tha pre-defined Second Approval Limit

Finance Manager : Included into Finance Manager(Accounting) group


Invoice will be into Third Approval state after second approval.

Director : Included into Director(Accounting) group


Invoice will be into open state after third approval.
